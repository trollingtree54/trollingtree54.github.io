Conflicts

David Leeming focusus on the supernatural in many of the tales he tells in his anthology. Supernatural conflict is very important to American culture and has helped start and maintain many traditions such as Halloween.

Another type of conflict that shows up a lot in the anthology is internal conflict. Internal conflicts usually focus on moral or ethical dillemas of characters. This makes it easier for authors to teach moral lessons to the reader as they are usually more direct with the message than other kinds of conflict.

Man vs Man is another kind of conflict used by David Leeming. The tale Billy the Kid uses this kind of conflict to retell the story of a battle fought in the 1800's in a small town in Lincoln County, NM. This kind of conflict makes it easier for authors to retell important historical events and make an exciting story at the same time.
