Summary

David Leeming's anthology "Myths, Legends, and Folktales of America" focusus on myths from many different cultures such as Asian American, European American, Native American, and African American. The anthology attempts to explain how the melting pot of American culture came to be by exploring different types of myths. Some categories it explores is creation myths, monster, trickster, and heros.
